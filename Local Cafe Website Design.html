Our Story

The six "old friends" who were all born in the 1980s met in the most beautiful times and have gone through 10 years of entrepreneurship together. They decided to work together again to create a place that evokes childhood memories, combining their collections and old toys collected from various places with the restaurant, sharing nostalgic memories with visitors, and stepping into the new era together.

The exterior adopts the theme of "Ama's Home", from the red bricks on the walls, the retro-printed floor tiles on the ground, to the rattan chairs placed outdoors. And the dark green door and window frames echo the word "age". Stepping into the room, the most eye-catching corner is the display stand on the right. There are many antique toys that can evoke childhood memories on the shelf, such as the old-fashioned electric fans, radios and matchboxes placed in the top row. They are all small objects that make people smile when they see it.
